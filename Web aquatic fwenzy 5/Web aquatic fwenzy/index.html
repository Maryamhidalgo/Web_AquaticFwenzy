Brave the Depths of a Forgotten Kingdom

In the tranquil depths of the ocean lies a hidden realm, veiled beneath the waves' shimmering embrace. Here, amidst the whispering currents and dancing light, lies a mysterious underwater kingdom waiting to be unveiled. As a courageous platypus armed with a unique gatling, you will embark on an extraordinary adventure.

Use Your Skills and Reflexes to Survive

Aquatic Fwenzy is an immersive  2D shoot'em up. You’ll explore diferrent Aquatic landscapes, engage in fierce battles against aquatic adversaries, and outsmart intricate obstacles, all to retrieve your offspring from the clutches of the malevolent sea serpent.

Mesmerizing Hand-Drawn Art

The underwater universe of Aquatic Fwenzy, where its depths are illuminated with a kaleidoscope of colors, is crafted in a charming 2D chibi style. Each new expanse you unveil is a distinct and wondrous spectacle, abounding with novel creatures and personages, all awaiting your discovery in the game’s ever-unfolding aquatic saga.
